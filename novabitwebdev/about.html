About Us

Novabit Capital was launched in 2021 to make disciplined investing easier to understand and easier to act on. Today, our global community includes more than 325,000 members learning how to build portfolios across crypto, stocks, ETFs, and broader market themes.

Our research team focuses on asset allocation, recurring investing, risk controls, and practical market education. Instead of pushing hype, we help members understand volatility, evaluate opportunities, and position for stronger long-term returns.

Because our platform relationships help support distribution and operations, we are able to keep a large share of our investor education and market resources free to access.

Your Gateway to Smarter Investing

Whether you are buying your first crypto position or refining a larger multi-asset portfolio, Novabit Capital is built to make investing clearer. We break down market structure, asset research, and portfolio construction into practical guidance that helps investors move with more confidence.

Start Building Your Investment Plan

Members get access to market commentary, portfolio ideas, live sessions, and educational resources covering diversification, recurring investing, custody, and risk management. Our goal is to help you build better habits that can compound into stronger results over time.
